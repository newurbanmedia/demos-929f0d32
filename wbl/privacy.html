Privacy policy

How we handle your information.

Last updated · April 2026

Works by Love is a small practice run by Edie Love. The information you share when you reach out, book a session, or work with Edie stays with Edie. This page explains what is collected, how it is used, and what your rights are. Plain language, no surprises.

What we collect

When you fill out the contact form on this site, the following is sent directly to Edie's inbox:

  • Your first and last name
  • Your email address
  • Your phone number
  • Anything you choose to write in the message field
  • The date and time the form was submitted

If you call or text the practice line, your phone number and the contents of your call or message are kept in Edie's phone records.

If you book a session, Edie will keep simple records of dates, location, and amount paid. Payment itself is processed by a third party (currently Square or Zelle) — Edie never sees or stores your full card information.

What we do with it

Your information is used only to:

  • Reply to your message or schedule a call
  • Coordinate sessions and follow up after them
  • Issue receipts and keep basic financial records

That is the entire list. Your information is not sold, shared, traded, given to advertisers, fed into a marketing tool, or used to train any model.

Confidentiality

What you say to Edie in a session is confidential. Edie does not record sessions, and she does not keep clinical notes. Names and details are not shared with other clients, family members, or anyone else without your explicit permission.

There are two narrow exceptions, both required by law:

  • If there is a clear and immediate risk of serious harm to you or someone else
  • If a court orders disclosure

Works by Love is grief counseling and practical support — it is not licensed psychotherapy or medical care. The same legal protections that apply to a licensed therapist may not apply here. If that distinction matters to you, please ask before booking.

Cookies and analytics

This website uses minimal cookies. There is no advertising, no third-party tracking, and no behavioral profiling. Basic, anonymous traffic statistics may be collected (page views, referrer, browser type) to understand whether the site is working.

How long we keep things

  • Contact form messages are kept until they are no longer useful, generally less than a year.
  • Client records (session dates, receipts) are kept for seven years for tax and accounting purposes.
  • Phone and text records are kept according to your phone carrier's policies and Edie's standard device retention.

Your rights

You can ask, at any time, to:

  • See what information Edie has about you
  • Correct anything that is wrong
  • Have your information deleted, except where Edie is required by law to keep it
  • Stop receiving any further communication from the practice

Email or call to make any of these requests. There is no form to fill out.

Changes to this policy

If this policy changes, the new version will be posted here with an updated date at the top. Material changes will be communicated to current clients directly.

Questions?

If anything here is unclear or you would like more detail before sending a message, please ask. Edie would rather answer a privacy question now than have you wonder later.
